- Your opponent's Knight is about to capture your Queen.
- Without her, your King is outgunned and you are doomed to lose.....*but what if she could fight back???*

  In Ultimate Chess, you have to WIN a war of cards to capture a piece.

  Otherwise, YOUR attacking piece dies. Your opponent's piece stands his ground.

  This repo is a sample [MadCap Flare](https://www.madcapsoftware.com/products/flare/) project where I outline the rules of Ultimate Chess.

  *It is also a work in progress using a 30-day trial. Hence the weird random characters throughout the outputs.*
